OUR PILOT PROJECT

NMIMS
Shirpur Campus

We successfully installed our first pilot system on 15th February 2018 at NMIMS Shirpur campus in Maharashtra which is stretched over 30 acres with 50 docks and 24 cycles at 7 Stations. The system is being used by 300 students and faculty which is constantly monitored for bugs and scope of improvement.
